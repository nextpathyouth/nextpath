Who We Are

Empowering the Next Generation

Our Story

NextPath Youth Empowerment Foundation was born out of a deep desire to bridge the gap between potential and opportunity for young people.

Our Vision

To raise a generation of empowered, responsible, and innovative young people who lead with purpose, integrity, and impact.

Our Mission

To equip teenagers and youths with mentorship, life skills, STEM education, and personal development opportunities.
